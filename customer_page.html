What Do Our Customers Look Like?

I first grouped the data by Customer ID and then filtered it to only show customers with more than 6 total transactions to create a data frame of repeat customers. I then created a column called 'birth_decade' which grouped all the customers by the decade in which they were born. The 'count' displays the sum of customers for each 'birth_decade'. We can see that customers born in the 1990's make up a much smaller portion of the total than the 70's and 80's. The company should find a way to target marketing towards this demographic.
When the repeat customers are grouped by "city_code" it becomes apparent that 0.0 has an almost non-existent cutomer base. The company should consider marketing more to that region to increase its total customers.
